General Thoughts

Maxwell is the only piece in my catalogue that followed me throughout my years at Berklee College of Music. I remember the first section (up until solos) came as a kind of divine inspiration off of a prompt from a groove writing course I took during my first few semesters at school. The piece was later used in a course that went through the basics of Protools where we needed to provide background material for a commerical of Maxwell's coffee. I had felt that this piece would fit perfectly with the atmosphere of the video commerical and opted to name the piece after the company. Thus Maxwell was fully realized.
