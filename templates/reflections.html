Reflections 2024 (c.a. 6 minutes)
for Solo Guitar




General Thoughts



For a while, I had been focusing on incorporating everything I've learned compositionally and utilizing that knowledge towards written music, all the while, my guitar sat collecting dust in the far corner of the room. I wanted to write a piece of music that a layman like myself could learn to play while still retaining the dense harmonic language of my previous pieces.



Instrumentation

Acoustic Guitar

* This piece was written for steel string guitar (Gretsch G9200 Boxcar Resonator)
